Welcome to OWA Mail Checker

This extension shows number of unread messages in you OWA-Account - also if you are not logged in!
The Number of unread messages will appear in the extensions icon.

Step 1

Navigate to your OWA-Login page. Copy the complete(!) URL of the page, paste it here and save.

Step 2

Your data is encrypted and only saved locally on this PC!
